How to run:
-Unzip file
-Run "make"
-Run "./plagiarismCatcher files/sm_doc_set 6 200"
  *Replace files/sm_doc_set with the desired directory,
   6 with the chunck size, and 200 with the number of
   matches necessary to consider possible plagiarism
-File will output sorted list of potential plagiarized files
 (may take close to 10 seconds for large sets)
